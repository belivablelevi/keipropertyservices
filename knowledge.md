## FAQ

**Q: How much does pressure washing cost in Ottawa?**
A: Kei Property Services starts pressure washing from $120. Final price depends on surface area and condition. All quotes are free.

**Q: What neighbourhoods do you serve in Ottawa?**
A: Primarily central Ottawa: Centretown, The Glebe, Westboro, Hintonburg, Sandy Hill, Vanier, Old Ottawa South, and Mechanicsville. Text us for areas not listed.

**Q: What is soft washing?**
A: Soft washing is a low-pressure cleaning method using biodegradable solutions. It is safer for delicate surfaces and kills mold and algae at the root rather than just blasting it off.

**Q: How do I get a quote?**
A: Fill out the form at keipropertyservices.ca/contact.html, call or text (343) 597-1646, or email keipropertyservices@gmail.com. All quotes are free.

**Q: Do you clean interlock driveways?**
A: Yes. Interlock, paver, concrete, and asphalt driveways are all cleaned.

**Q: How long does pressure washing take?**
A: A standard driveway takes 1–3 hours. A full exterior property package may take half a day.

**Q: Do you work in the rain?**
A: Light rain is generally fine for pressure washing. We will advise if weather requires rescheduling.

**Q: Can pressure washing damage my surfaces?**
A: When done correctly, no. Kei Property Services uses appropriate pressure settings for each surface type and uses soft washing for delicate surfaces.

**Q: Do I need to be home during the job?**
A: Not necessarily. We can arrange access and send photos when complete.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Contact us at (343) 597-1646 to confirm current payment options.
