Who We Are

One student.
One serious business.

Kei Services started with a simple observation: most property service companies in Ottawa treat customer communication and presentation as an afterthought. We decided to fix that.

Kei Services is built by a high school student who wanted to build something real while young enough to put in the work. This is not a side hustle. We show up with proper equipment, communicate throughout every job, and send before-and-after photos when done.
