Who We Are

Two students.
One serious business.

Kei Services started with a simple observation: most property service companies in Ottawa treat customer communication and presentation as an afterthought. We decided to fix that.

We are two high school students who wanted to build something real while we were young enough to put in the work. We are not a side hustle. We show up with proper equipment, we communicate throughout every job, and we send before-and-after photos when we are done.

The kei truck came first. Everything else followed from there.

The Truck

Why a kei truck?

A Japanese kei truck is not a typical choice for a property services company in Ottawa. That is exactly the point.

Kei trucks are compact, capable, and built for utility work in tight urban environments. They fit into narrow driveways, gated backyards, and access roads that full-size pickup trucks cannot reach. That opens up jobs that other crews turn down.

But honestly, we also just love the thing. It looks different, it moves different, and it makes a statement about what kind of company we are trying to build. One that thinks about the details.
