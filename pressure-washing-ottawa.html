Ottawa Climate

Why Ottawa properties need annual pressure washing.

Ottawa sees some of the most punishing seasonal swings of any Canadian city. Winters bring road salt, ice, and freeze-thaw cycles that work grime deep into concrete and interlock. Spring brings moisture that accelerates mold and algae growth on every surface. Summer humidity keeps it growing.

The result: by the time most Ottawa homeowners notice the black driveway, green fence, or grey deck — the buildup has been there for years. Annual pressure washing prevents that accumulation before it becomes a restoration project.

The best time to pressure wash in Ottawa is spring (April–June) after salt and debris clears, or fall (August–October) before the freeze locks in another season of grime.

Spring Cleaning
Post-winter reset

Remove road salt, sand, winter grime, and the algae that grows under snow. Best for driveways, walkways, and siding.

Fall Cleaning
Pre-winter preparation

Clear summer mold and algae buildup before it freezes into surfaces. Decks, fences, and patios respond best in fall.

Pre-Sale
Maximum curb appeal

Ottawa realtors consistently list exterior cleaning as the highest-ROI pre-sale improvement. One day of pressure washing can add thousands to a first impression.

FAQ

Common questions about
pressure washing in Ottawa.

How much does pressure washing cost in Ottawa?
Kei Property Services offers pressure washing starting from $120 in Ottawa. The final price depends on the surface type, size, and level of buildup. All quotes are free and provided within 24 hours with no obligation.
What surfaces can you pressure wash in Ottawa?
We pressure wash driveways (concrete, asphalt, interlock, paver), decks (wood, composite, PVC), patios, walkways, steps, fences (wood, vinyl, chain link), house siding, and retaining walls throughout Ottawa.
What is the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?
Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to physically remove dirt and grime. Soft washing uses lower pressure combined with biodegradable cleaning solutions that kill mold and algae at the root. Soft washing is safer for delicate surfaces like siding and painted wood, and the results last longer.
When is the best time to pressure wash in Ottawa?
Spring (April–June) and fall (August–October) are the best times for Ottawa properties. Spring removes road salt, sand, and winter grime. Fall clears summer mold and algae before the freeze bonds it to surfaces over winter.
How long does pressure washing take?
A standard driveway takes 1–3 hours depending on size. A deck takes 1–2 hours. A full exterior package (driveway, deck, walkways, fence) may take half a day. We give you a time estimate when we quote the job.
Do I need to be home during the job?
Not necessarily. We can arrange access and will send you before-and-after photos when the job is complete. Discuss access arrangements when you book.
Will pressure washing damage my interlock or driveway?
When done at the correct pressure, no. Kei Property Services adjusts the water pressure and angle based on the surface material. Interlock and paver driveways are cleaned without displacing the jointing sand.
Do you provide before-and-after photos?
Yes. Every job is documented with before-and-after photos that are sent directly to you on completion. You see the full transformation.
